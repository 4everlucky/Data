Atuba Cheif Varkees:
The almighty Flame Lord has ordered me, Varkees, to defeat the disgusting rakeclaw imps. Young Orc, have you experience in fighting against rakeclaw imps? They are creatures created from the power of black magic. These blasphemous creatures are defiling our holy land even as we speak!
