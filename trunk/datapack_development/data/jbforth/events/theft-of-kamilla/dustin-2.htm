Dustin

Thank you! You are so lavish knight! So, it became known, that the one stranger knows where is lady Kamilla. His name is Trash. He hides hereabout. I think, he is still in the Giran. You should find him and ask where and who had concealed lady Kamilla. After the rescuing of lady Kamilla return to the High Priest Maximillian for the reward. May the Einhasad help you!
